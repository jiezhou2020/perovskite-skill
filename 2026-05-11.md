## I. STABILITY

### PROBLEM（加具体数字 + 25 yr 锚点）

> Devices are vulnerable under humidity, heat, light, and electric-field stress, undergoing ion migration, phase segregation, and interfacial degradation. Best certified T80 lifetimes reach **~3 000 h under ISOS-L-3** (85 °C, 60 % RH) — still well below the **25-year (~10⁵ h)** benchmark set by silicon.

### RESEARCH DIRECTIONS（最后一项更具体）

- Compositional engineering: FA / Cs mixed cations to suppress the α → δ transition.
- Interfacial passivation: 2D / 3D heterostructures and self-assembled monolayers (SAMs).
- Encapsulation: atomic-layer deposition (ALD) and polymer composite barriers.
- **Standardised ISOS protocols (D-1, L-2, T-3) + failure-mechanism modelling for cross-lab comparison.**

## II. UPSCALING

### PROBLEM（补 area gap 数字）

> Moving from cm² lab devices to m² modules, crystallisation kinetics, wet-film uniformity, and defect density all deteriorate sharply with area. Best certified **module efficiency (~24 %) lags lab cells (26.7 %) by 3–5 % absolute**, and yield falls with both area and process complexity.

### RESEARCH DIRECTIONS（保留 + 加 in-line QC）

- Scalable coating: blade coating, slot-die coating, inkjet printing.
- Adapting antisolvent / gas-quench crystallisation to large areas.
- Module interconnects: optimised P1–P2–P3 laser scribing.
- Process transfer from spin-coating toward roll-to-roll manufacturing.
- **In-line metrology (PL / XRD) for real-time crystallisation QC.**（可选第 5 条）

### Bottom metric

- 现版 "≥ 800 cm² MODULE-SCALE RESEARCH TARGET" — 合理
- 升级版："**10.5 cm² @ 23 %** mini-module (Yu 2026); industry target ≥ 800 cm²"

## III. EFFICIENCY ⭐（重点：补 tandem 衔接）

### PROBLEM（补 tandem 桥）

> Single-junction PSCs at **26.7 % certified efficiency** are converging on the Shockley–Queisser ceiling (~33 % at optimum bandgap). Non-radiative recombination, parasitic absorption, and transport-layer mismatch cap further gains in V_OC and fill factor. **Breaking this ceiling requires tandem architectures — perovskite/Si and all-perovskite stacks — which demand a stable wide-bandgap (1.65–1.75 eV) top cell, the focus of the next slide.**

### RESEARCH DIRECTIONS（重写第 3 条让 tandem 更突出）

- Defect passivation: Lewis acid/base chemistry, ammonium-salt post-treatments.
- Contact / transport optimisation: SAM-based HTLs, low-recombination ETLs.
- **Tandem architectures: perovskite/Si (34.9 % LONGi 2024), all-perovskite (30.1 %), P/CIGS (30.7 %) — all need stable WBG top cell.**
- Light management: textured Si interfaces, anti-reflection coatings.

## Tandem 叙事的关键句（口述用）

讲到 Efficiency 这一列时，建议口述这样过渡到 slide 5：

> Single-junction perovskites are saturating against the Shockley–Queisser limit. The **only** physically motivated path to ≥ 30 % is multi-junction stacking, and the most mature route — perovskite/silicon tandem — has now reached 34.9 % under LONGi's 2024 result. **But all of this depends on a stable wide-bandgap top cell**, and wide-bandgap mixed-halide perovskites suffer from a specific instability — phase segregation — which is the focus of the next slide.
